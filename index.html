 X assigned names and numbers authority


Note:

This and the other documents here are meant to be used as templates for a simple certificate and assigned names/numbers authority, along with the registry that it publishes. The "X" stands for the name of the organizationr sponsoring the CA and registry.

The xANNA registry

The registry is essentially a hierarchical filesystem using naming conventions to facilitate browsing and programmatic access.
